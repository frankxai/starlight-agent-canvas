# Competitor Notes

This product should be positioned as a local-first agent substrate, not as a clone.

## Benchmarks

- Poppy AI: visual creative workspace benchmark. Public positioning emphasizes drag/drop sources such as YouTube videos, podcasts, PDFs, voice notes, images, websites, docs, and social video links, plus mind-map/editor workflows.
- Nodeflow AI: source-connected visual research board benchmark. Public positioning emphasizes connecting YouTube, Instagram, TikTok, PDFs, and websites to ChatGPT/Claude-style analysis and content workflows.
- AI Flow Chat: reusable workflow and app packaging benchmark.
- Superly: social/viral research benchmark.
- Miro: general collaboration canvas benchmark.

## Differentiation

- OSS-first.
- MCP-native.
- Local data by default.
- Agent-readable and agent-writable canvases.
- Portable JSON/Markdown artifacts.
- Safe by design: no external posting or destructive tools in v0.1.

## v0.1 Response

- Match the source-intake expectation locally for URLs, YouTube links, PDFs, transcripts, text files, and raw notes.
- Preserve the Starlight wedge: MCP-native, OSS-first, local data, portable exports, and no posting/destructive actions.
- Defer social-platform ingestion and collaboration until the source artifact model, mobile controls, and provider adapters are stronger.
