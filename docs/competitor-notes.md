# Competitor Notes

This product should be positioned as a local-first agent substrate, not as a clone.

## Benchmarks

- Poppy AI: visual creative workspace benchmark.
- Nodeflow AI: source-connected visual research board benchmark.
- AI Flow Chat: reusable workflow and app packaging benchmark.
- Superly: social/viral research benchmark.
- Miro: general collaboration canvas benchmark.

## Differentiation

- OSS-first.
- MCP-native.
- Local data by default.
- Agent-readable and agent-writable canvases.
- Portable JSON/Markdown artifacts.
- Safe by design: no external posting or destructive tools in v0.1.
